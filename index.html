About Us

BoxCamp is the answer to enjoying your summer, faster. That starts with giving you the easiest moving experience you’ll ever have. Whether you can’t afford to take everything home or just don’t want to, send your belongings to BoxCamp and go enjoy your stress-free summer!

Order

No hidden fees. No Taxes. Free boxes.
Choose your storage package or select individual items on your school page. We’ll even store your microwave, mini fridge, and bicycle. All orders are a flat price all summer.

Pick Up Materials

You can pick up your free boxes before finals start. We’ll put them together for you right on campus. Visit our Packing Tips page to see what to store and how to store it!

Drop-Off and Enjoy!

Bring your packed belongings to our convenient locations right on campus. With a quick last few steps, you’ll be on your way to enjoying your summer!
