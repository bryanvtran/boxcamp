Packing Tips

fast
easy to use
affordable
made by students for students

Here are a few packing tips to ensure that your items are stored properly and everything is ready to go when dropping off!

Please ensure that none of your items including suitcases and dressers weigh over 40 lb or 18 kg. Overweight items will be rejected and need to be repacked.

You may use your own boxes. Please do not store boxes with BoxCamp that are different sizes from our boxes. BoxCamp boxes are 18x18x18 and 24x18x18.

Ensure to legibly write your name, order number, return day, and box # of total on each box.

Spread textbooks between multiple boxes and towards the middle of the box to prevent damaged items and crushed boxes.

Tape dresser drawers shut completely and DO NOT store plastic dressers that will break when lifted.

Please do not store food, liquids, valuables, perishable foods, or (we have to say it) weapons, illegal drugs, or paraphernalia in your boxes or items.

Do not OVERPACK your boxes, suitcase, or dresser. Bulging boxes and dressers will be rejected and need to be repacked.

Do not UNDERPACK your boxes. Ensure to try to fill the space in each box adequately and to balance out the weight.
